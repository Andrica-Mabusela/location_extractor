## Who is Vukile?
[More about vukile](https://www.vukile.co.za/about-us/)
Vukile Property Fund Limited is a high-quality, low-risk, specialist retail real estate investment trust (REIT) with a geographically diversified portfolio of property assets valued at R64 billion, of which 67% is based in the Iberian Peninsula.
